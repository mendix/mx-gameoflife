This is an implementation of Conway's Game of Life.
The basic rules are,
  • A cell with less than two live neighbours dies (loneliness)
  • A cell with more than three live neighbours dies (starvation)
  • A cell with two or three live neighbours lives
  • A dead cell with three live neighbours comes to life
